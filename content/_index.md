## Welcome!

Hi, I'm Gus. Thanks for stopping by.

This site is where I write about the things I build, the problems I run into, and the solutions that seem worth keeping. Most of it sits somewhere around infrastructure as code, Azure, automation, and small software projects with a practical purpose.

I like tools that are clear, dependable, and easy to operate. That usually pulls me toward repeatable infrastructure, lightweight services, and notes that explain not just what changed, but why.

Some posts here are about cloud and deployment work. Others are about side projects, data experiments, or the small decisions that make a system easier to understand and run.

I am not trying to present any of this as the one right way to do things. It is mostly a record of what I tried, what worked, and what I learned along the way.

If that sounds like your kind of internet, have a look around. You can start with the [blog](/blog/) or read a bit more [about me](/about/).
